Theorem 2 Sum of two even numbers is even
✓

Let \(a, b \in \mathbb {N}\). If \(a\) is even and \(b\) is even, then \(a + b\) is even.

Proof ▼

Since \(a\) is even, there exists \(j \in \mathbb {N}\) with \(a = 2j\). Since \(b\) is even, there exists \(k \in \mathbb {N}\) with \(b = 2k\). Then

\[ a + b = 2j + 2k = 2(j + k), \]

so \(a + b\) is even.
